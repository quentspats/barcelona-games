The games

Four challenges, one finale, zero spectators.

Every game is short, mixed, and built to scramble teams together. You will play, sweat, laugh, and be back at your flag in 25 minutes.

🚩~25 min including setup and reset

Flag Relay

A relay race where the team flag never stops moving.

Objective
First team to bring its flag through every station and back to base wins.
Team size
5–6 per team

How it works

  • Runner 1 sprints with the flag to station 1, does 5 burpees, hands off to runner 2.
  • Runner 2 carries the flag through a slalom of cones to station 2.
  • Runner 3 must hop on one foot to station 3 holding the flag overhead.
Scoring · 1st = 4 pts · 2nd = 3 · 3rd = 2 · 4th = 1.
Why it connects · Forces fast hand-offs, eye contact, names shouted at full volume — instant ice-breaker.
🙈~30 min

Blind Trust Course

One blindfold, four voices, one obstacle course.

Objective
Guide a blindfolded teammate through a sand obstacle course faster than the other teams.
Team size
4 per round, full team rotates

How it works

  • Only the three callers can speak. The runner cannot remove the blindfold.
  • Touching a cone = +5 second penalty. Stepping in the lava zone = restart from last cone.
  • Each team rotates so everyone takes a turn blindfolded.
Scoring · Lowest cumulative time across rotations wins. 4-3-2-1 across teams.
Why it connects · You learn names instantly because you have to shout them clearly and trust them blindly.
🥚~20 min

Beach Toss (Egg or Water Balloon)

Last pair to drop the toss is your team's hero.

Objective
Pairs throw and catch a water balloon (or raw egg if you are brave). Step back after every successful catch.
Team size
Pairs from each team

How it works

  • Drop or burst = pair is out.
  • Last pair on the beach wins the round.
  • Best of 3 rounds, teams send different pairs each round.
Scoring · Each round-win earns the team 3 pts. Bonus 1 pt for biggest splash.
Why it connects · Makes everyone laugh, gets pairs to coordinate, and the splash zone always pulls a crowd.
🏐~25 min

Spikeball Team Rotation

Mini 2v2 matches, fast rotations, every player plays.

Objective
Win as many quick-fire 2v2 matches as your team can in 25 minutes.
Team size
Pairs rotating; everyone plays at least 2 matches

How it works

  • Standard spikeball rules: hit the net, max 3 touches per side.
  • First to 7, must win by 2. Hard cap at 9.
  • Loser pair sits, new pair from same team rotates in.
Scoring · Every win = 2 pts for the team. Tie-break: total points scored across all matches.
Why it connects · The high-rotation format means everyone plays with people they have not yet met.
