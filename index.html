Why it works

Three reasons it converts strangers into friends.

01

Meet your team in 5 minutes.

Tie on a bandana, walk to your colour. Within five minutes you have a name, a chant, and four teammates to shout with.

02

Surprise games, real connection.

Different skills every round (strategy, teamwork, trust, coordination). The chaos is what makes the bonding stick.

03

Walk out with a new crew.

Medals, photos, awards, then a walk to a tapas bar. By the time you stand up, the WhatsApp group is already buzzing.
