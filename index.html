Why it works

Three reasons it converts strangers into friends.

01

Meet your team in 5 minutes.

You come alone. We've already built your team. On arrival, we hand you a bandana and a quick brief: meet your four teammates, pick a team name, write your chant — plus a couple of little surprises we slip you to break the ice. By the first whistle, your team already feels like a team. Coming with friends? Tell us — we keep you together or spread you across teams so the whole crew meets new people.

02

Surprise games, real connection.

Different skills every round (strategy, teamwork, trust, coordination). The chaos is what makes the bonding stick.

03

Walk out with a new crew.

Medals, photos, awards, then a walk to a beach bar for pica-pica. By the time you stand up, the WhatsApp group is already buzzing.

The bandana system

Five teams. Five Barcelona icons.

On arrival, we hand you a printed bandana and point you at your team's colored flag on the sand. Each team is named after a Barcelona landmark. Walk to your flag. Four teammates are already there.

  • Bandana = team. Wear it on your head, wrist, or shoulder.
  • Each team gets a big colored flag planted on the sand.
  • First task with your new team: a name and a chant.
