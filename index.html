The flag system

Walk to your colour.

After check-in we send you straight to your team's big colored flag on the sand. By the time your sandals come off, you already know who you're with.

  • Wristband matches flag — spot teammates from anywhere.
  • Each flag is your basecamp for the whole day.
  • First task: agree on a team name and chant.
